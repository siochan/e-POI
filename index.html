e-POI

E-POI will incentivize the users to collect accurate data by a play-to-earn approach and cryptocurrency-based rewards. The project will use blockchain to secure immutable POI data. The process involves walking to a point of interest, 3D scanning the location, verifying the data, and converting it into a digital token that you will receive as a reward.

e-POI provides a streamlined process of collecting and updating point of interest (POI) data.

-Decentralizes POI data collection

-Improves POI data quality

-Incentivizes users

-Captures multi-layered POI Data
