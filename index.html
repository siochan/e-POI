e-POI

Our project aims to collect and update data to provide mental health resources to our target population. We are working to help provide relevant information, resources, and locations for mental health resources for individuals that are between the ages of 18-29.

e-POI provides a streamlined process of collecting and updating point of interest (POI) data.

-Decentralizes POI data collection

-Improves POI data quality

-Incentivizes users

-Captures multi-layered POI Data

Process

Step One

Earn Cryptocurrency and collect digital assets

Step Two

Scan locations to earn NFTs

Step Three

Keep scanning locations to earn badges and achievements
